About me

I am the Web Developer focused on the front-end. Why front-end? The answer is, it integrates my personal traits in one single passion. As an artist, I like playing with a design; and as an engineer, I love solving logical problems and writing code. Famous saying by Einstein, 'Logic will take you from A to B. Imagination will take you everywhere', is my maxim for life.

I am fascinated by new frameworks and libraries, however, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript are my all time favorites and offer a great power on their own. To mention one example, ability to use them without help of extra libraries optimizes the performance by a great deal.

Technologies I love to code with : HTML, CSS/Sass, JavaScript/ES6+, Node.js, React

Tools and skills I use for coding: VS Code, Git/Github, PostgreSQL, RESTful APIs...and the last but not least - Google and a rubber duck!
